Gold Membership

Cost: US$100 per year
Benefits:

  • Advertisments posted four times a week in the spotlights
  • 50% event discounts

Silver Membership

Cost: US$50 per year
Benefits:

  • Access to half of business capacitations

Bronze Membership

Cost: US$40 per year
Benefits:

  • Access to quarter of business capacitations
  • Advertisments posted three times a week in the spotlights
  • 30% event discounts

Non Profit Membership

Cost: No cost
Benefits:

  • Access to all of business capacitations

  • 40% event discounts
