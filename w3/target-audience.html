Target audience

The target audience is the people who want to join the Luján Chamber of Commerce and
those who already are members to grow their business, mantain them notified of new issues about Commerce
and the country economic course and more. Also for the people who are not members of the organization:
creating this website helps to see the people who are not involved to know about what we are doing and
all the benefits of the existence of this entity.
